A little more about Ken

Journalism Educator and Scholar

I am a PhD candidate in the E. W. Scripps School of Journalism at Ohio University. I'll finish my dissertation, a history of the more than 100 years of competition between the Denver Post and Rocky Mountain News, in February 2018.

I'm the proud husband of my high school sweetheart, with whom I've been friends since moving to the same Colorado town of 800 residents in third grade, and father of the cutest baby girl ever born.
